📱 Mobile Tile Size Improvements

✨ What's Been Improved

The number tiles in Fancy2048 are now significantly larger and more readable on mobile devices!

🔧 Technical Changes:

  • Mobile Phones (≤480px): Font scales increased by 25-50%
  • Small Phones (≤360px): Even larger fonts for better readability
  • Tablets (≤768px): Enhanced scaling for medium-sized screens
  • Landscape Mobile: Optimized for horizontal orientation

📐 Responsive Improvements by Device Size

📱 Small Phones (≤360px width)
Font scaling increased to 0.48-0.60 (was 0.32-0.45)
Perfect for older iPhones and compact Android devices
📱 Mobile Phones (≤480px width)
Font scaling increased to 0.42-0.55 (was 0.32-0.45)
Optimized for most modern smartphones
📱 Landscape Mobile (≤768px width, ≤500px height)
Font scaling increased to 0.34-0.50 (was default)
Better readability when phone is rotated
📱 Tablets (≤768px width)
Enhanced font scaling: 0.38-0.60 (was 0.30-0.50)
Improved for iPad and Android tablets

🎯 Grid Size Specific Improvements

  • 3x3 Grid: Largest font increases - perfect for beginners
  • 4x4 Grid: Balanced scaling - optimal for most players
  • 5x5 Grid: Smart scaling - maintains readability with more tiles
  • 7x7+ Grids: Proportional increases - keeps advanced play accessible
